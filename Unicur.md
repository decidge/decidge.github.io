**Decentralized Decidge (DD)** is a novel global financial architecture where the **UCR (Unicur)** token serves as the digital equivalent of ownership rights to the means of production.

At launch, UCR is offered at a fixed rate of **1 unit = $1**. All proceeds are allocated exclusively to the **Asset Pool** for the acquisition, modernization, and automation of real-world production assets (factories, data centers, energy, logistics, etc.). The protocol becomes the direct owner of these assets.

Each unit acquired automatically confers a proportional ownership stake in the entire asset pool. This is recorded in an **immutable Ledger** as a legal event. Token holders are entitled to:

- **Dividends** / a share of production income;
- **Governance rights** (voting on new asset acquisitions and strategic direction).

The **total supply** is capped at **210 trillion units** (benchmarked against global GDP and broad money supply). Initially, only a minimal share (**1–3%**) will be in circulation, with the remainder locked under long-term **vesting (10–20 years)**. Unlocking is tethered proportionally to the actual growth of the protocol’s assets and GDP.

Following the initial phase, the exchange rate shifts to market-based pricing, while the token’s **intrinsic value** remains anchored to real assets (**NAV** — Net Asset Value per unit). This creates a virtuous cycle: _investment → production growth → asset value appreciation → increased token intrinsic value → further investment._

Ownership of the means of production is protected by **anti-monopolization barriers** (e.g., a 10–20% cap on any single enterprise or asset class). The Ledger operates above national jurisdictions as a **new layer of trust and ownership**—functioning in parallel with traditional registries, yet faster, more transparent, and resilient to corruption and sanctions.

This is more than just a token.

It is a **digital claim on future production** for an era where money as a measure of labor ceases to exist, leaving ownership of the means of production as the ultimate foundation of value.
